# About me
---
I am Penghang Shuai(帅鹏航), I received the M.E. degree in Artificial Intelligence with the Institute of Automation(supervised by Prof.Haipeng Li) with the Laboratory of Cognition and Decision Intelligence for Complex Systems, Chinese Academy of Sciences. And I graduated from Chengdu University of Technology with the B.E. degree in Electrical Engineering and Automation in 2022.

I really hope to do good work in the field of robotics. My interests include robotics, reinforcement learning, and robot perception.
